About Us

Our Firm

For years, I, Melissa Basehoar, CPA (Sole Member of All Things Tax & Accounting, LLC) has been providing quality, personalized financial guidance to local individuals and businesses. All Things Tax & Accounting's expertise includes basic tax return preparation, tax planning, and accounting/bookkeeping services. All Things Tax & Accounting, LLC is one of the leading firms in and throughout the North Strand area.

By combining my expertise, experience and the team mentality of my staff, I assure that every client receives the close analysis and attention they deserve. My dedication to high standards, hiring of seasoned tax professionals, and work ethic is the reason my client base returns year after year.

Our Mission

My mission is to help clients maintain financial viability in the present, while taking a proactive approach to achieve future goals. This requires open communication to reach an understanding of my clients' needs through research and sound analysis. All Things Tax & Accounting, LLC is dedicated to meeting these goals with high standards of excellence and professionalism. I have been a staple of the North Strand business community for years, and pride myself on the level of esteem my practice has earned.

My dedication to hard work has earned the respect of the business and financial community in and around the North Strand area. I believe this to be a direct derivative of the talent and responsiveness of the staff employed. Whether you are a current or prospective client, rest assured that individuals and businesses who choose All Things Tax & Accounting, LLC receive competent and timely advice.
