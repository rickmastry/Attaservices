Services

Federal/State Income Tax Returns

When it comes to taxes, both personal and business, proper planning is the key to financial success. At All Things Tax & Accounting, our tax professionals know how to maximize your tax benefits, while also avoiding future problems. We take the time to understand each client's individual situation and then apply our knowledge of the changing tax environment to help you make the best business and personal decisions. We constantly monitor tax legislation, regulations and court rulings so we can provide the most effective tax planning for the future of your business or your personal finances.

We also understand the importance of filing your taxes correctly and on time. Our experts can efficiently prepare your tax return. We can then review what you've done in the past and offer ideas and options to lower your taxes in the future.

Sales Tax Returns

State and local taxes, especially sales taxes, have become a significant cost of doing business as states scramble for additional revenues. Compliance has become very cumbersome and costly for businesses. Companies need customized solutions tailored to their unique business needs, whether they are a Fortune 500 company or just getting started.

At All Things Tax & Accounting, our professionals focus on providing customized solutions by working with your company and uncovering the nuances of your business. We blend your unique situation with our tax and systems knowledge to address your company's specific sales tax issues.

Full Payroll Services

We believe that our payroll clients deserve more than just computer generated checks and reports with no guidance or personal service. We have an in-depth understanding of the payroll needs and issues for business owners. We provide our clients with guidance on all payroll matters. This also includes quarterly returns and W-2s.
